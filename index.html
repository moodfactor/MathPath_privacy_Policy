Privacy Policy for Math Path

Effective Date: January 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Math Path collects, uses, and protects user data. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information depending on how you use the app:

  • User Profile Data: Name, grade level, or avatar (if a profile is created).
  • Usage Data: Progress, scores, number of sessions, challenges completed.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, crash reports.
  • Third-party Analytics: Anonymous usage stats for improving the app.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide personalized learning and game progress
  • Track user performance and app usage trends
  • Improve our content and educational features
  • Send optional updates or in-app notifications

3. Third-Party Services

Math Path may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (for authentication, real-time database, crash reporting)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • AdMob (if ads are displayed – optional)

These services may collect anonymized data according to their own policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Math Path is designed with children's privacy in mind. We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to safeguard user data, including encryption and secure access protocols.

6. User Rights

You can contact us to:

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date.
